About Us

Altius Global Education Consultants is a premier overseas education facilitator, headquartered in Thrissur, Kerala, India. Established in the year 1999, AGEC is family based startup took shape from the strong conviction of its management that new age leaders armed with world class education can only transform tomorrowâ€™s world, which motivated him to set up of a credible and supportive overseas education facilitation enterprise to offer the very best learning opportunities worldwide to smart, young Indian minds. complementing the management is a team of highly motivated professionals with years of experience and skill.
Altius Global Education Constultants is a leader in connecting people to abroad education facilities and jobs.For more than 20 years, AGEC has helped people improve their lives by providing better oppertunity as they needed. Today, the company offers services in more than 10 countries,providing some of the broadest,most sophisticated career management and talent management capacities

Mission

To improve exposure and accessibilty to national/international educational prospects, across the process from information to application

Vission

To foster global exposure among aspiring students . We believe that quality guidance can be affordable and even free for the truly aspiring. It is the dream we work towards.

Why Us?

With 20 years of heritage in educational services sector, we are determined to change educational prospects are seen and known today. Fostering student communities across the globe by providing guidance in national and international competitive examinations,Altius Global Education Consultants is your reliable education facilitator
